About Me

Hi, I’m Anna Theresa and I’m an Educational Coordinator, Mentor, Wrestler and Pageant Queen.

As an Educational Coordinator and Mentor with The Child Center of NY, I believe it is extremely important to provide counseling, educational, health and family support for our community. Please click here to find out more about what we do or donate as well!

Pageanting is something I love doing as I find it inspirational, uplifting and honorable. It is a great platform to talk about current issues and to help promote The Child Center of NY. Please click here to follow my pageant page.

Wrestling is a wonderful passion I discovered as a fan. I have now become a dedicated performer and wrestling is a big part of my life. It's a great way to boost confidence, get that cardio going and is just plain fun! Click here to see any performances I may have coming up.

Thanks for stopping by!

-Anna

The Child Center of New York

Working as an Educational Coordinator at the The Child Center of New York for the Neighborhood Development Area Program has been a transformative experience. I also mentor with the Success Mentoring Initiative and will be spearheading The Dropout Prevention and Re-engagement Program in Far Rockaway, NY. Working towards a better future for our youth is extremely important to me and that is what drives us at The Child Center of New York. Our mission is to strengthen children and families with skills, opportunities, and emotional support to build healthy, successful lives.
